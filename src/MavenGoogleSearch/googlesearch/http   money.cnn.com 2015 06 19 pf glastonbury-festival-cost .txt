      <h1 class="article-title">How much it really costs to party at Glastonbury</h1> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
    <div class="row"> 
     <div class="column"> 
      <!-- main --> 
      <div class="share-tools pull-left" id="js-sharebar-main"></div> 
      <div class="pull-right"> 
       <span class="cnnbyline js-vid-byline-"> <span class="byline">By Ellen Thompson and Alanna Petroff </span>&nbsp; <a href="https://twitter.com/intent/user?screen_name=CNNMoney" class="soc-twtname">@CNNMoney</a> </span> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
    </div> 
   </header> 
   <div class="row two-columns-fixed-rr"> 
    <section class="column"> 
     <article class="module"> 
      <div id="storycontent"> 
       <!--storytext--> 
       <div id="storytext"> 
        <figure id="ie_dottop"> 
         <img src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/money/dam/assets/150618115806-glastonbury-festival-party-concert-780x439.jpg" width="780" height="439" alt="glastonbury festival party concert" border="0" /> 
         <figcaption class="caption">
          Glastonbury is turning 45 this year but is still the hottest festival in the U.K.
         </figcaption> 
        </figure> 
        <div class="share-tools share-tools--floater" id="js-sharebar-floater"></div> 
        <h2>The mother of all British music festivals starts next week, with 135,000 revelers set to descend on Glastonbury for the five-day extravaganza.</h2> 
        <p> Tickets sold out in October in just 25 minutes, at &pound;225 ($360) per person. </p> 
        <div id="ie_column"> 
         <div id="ie_column"> 
          <div id="quigo220">
           <!-- ADSPACE: technology/quigo/ctr.220x200 --> 
           <div id="ad_ns_btf_03"></div> 
           <!-- ADSPACE-END -->
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <p> That may sound steep, but the price of attendance can be many times that amount for those who want to live it up. </p> 
        <p> Here's the breakdown: </p> 
        <p> <strong>Travel: </strong>A coach ticket from London to Glastonbury -- roughly a 135 mile drive -- will cost about &pound;53 ($84) for a round trip. </p> 
        <p> Dedicated Glasto-fans who fly into the U.K. from abroad will obviously be paying a heck of a lot more for flights. </p> 
        <p> A-listers wanting to grab a helicopter to skip the festival traffic jams will have to pay around &pound;860 ($1,370) per person for a flight from nearby Bristol. </p> 
        <p> <strong>Accommodation:</strong> There are no hotel options here. Everyone at Glastonbury has to camp. </p> 
        <p> For those on a tight budget, a basic tent, sleeping bag and air mattress can cost as little as &pound;55 ($87). </p> 
        <p> Those who want a better night's sleep can opt for a luxury &quot;yurt&quot; from <a href="http://www.landnsky.co.uk/index.php" target="_blank">Land&amp;Sky</a>, which costs nearly &pound;9,500 ($15,100) for four people. (Fancy food is included with this option, and staff will pack picnics to keep you going in the mosh pit.) </p> 
        <figure class="body_img body_img--780"> 
         <img src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/money/dam/assets/150618113341-glastonbury-festival-tents-cost-780x439.jpg" width="780" height="439" alt="glastonbury festival tents cost" border="0" /> 
         <figcaption class="caption">
          Tents and camping are part of the Glastonbury tradition.
         </figcaption> 
        </figure> 
        <p> </p> 
        <p> <strong>Gear: </strong>Glastonbury organizers advise attendees to bring a reusable water bottle, sunscreen, toilet paper, wet wipes and condoms. </p> 
        <p> Wise festival goers also know to bring a flashlight for nighttime bathroom trips and midnight excursions. </p> 
        <p> And you'll probably want to bring it all in a sturdy backpack. </p> 
        <p> If you buy everything yourself, you could probably get it for as little as &pound;40 ($64). But splurging is always an option. </p> 
        <p> <strong>Clothing: </strong>Rain boots -- aka Wellies -- are a <i>must</i> at these British festivals, which are known for their rain and muck. </p> 
        <p> A basic pair can cost as little at &pound;10 ($16), while the trendy Hunter brand sells boots for as much as &pound;175 ($280). </p> 
        <figure class="body_img body_img--780"> 
         <img src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/money/dam/assets/150618111103-glastonbury-boots-780x439.jpg" width="780" height="439" alt="glastonbury boots" border="0" /> 
         <figcaption class="caption">
          Glasto-fans all bring rain boots to navigate through the mud. (Brits call them &quot;wellies.&quot;)
         </figcaption> 
        </figure> 
        <p> </p> 
        <p> Raincoats are also essential, and prices can vary widely. </p> 
        <p> Many people also like to purchase special clothing and accessories for the event, including boho-chic tops, flowery head bands and face paint. </p> 
        <p> British stores stock up on these items during festival season so you can easily find a deal. </p> 
        <figure class="body_img body_img--780"> 
         <img src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/money/dam/assets/150618114440-glastonbury-festival-concert-party-crowd-780x439.jpg" width="780" height="439" alt="glastonbury festival concert party crowd" border="0" /> 
         <figcaption class="caption">
          Glastonbury in full swing.
         </figcaption> 
        </figure> 
        <p> </p> 
        <p> <strong>Food and drink:</strong> Never underestimate the cost of food and beverages. </p> 
        <p> Those on a budget will likely be stocking up on granola bars. </p> 
        <p> Buying food at the festival can quickly add up. Four donuts sell for &pound;4.50 ($7.15), or you can treat yourself to a lobster meal for &pound;25 ($40). </p> 
        <p> As for drinks, it all depends on how well you want to remember the festival! </p> 
        <figure class="body_img body_img--780"> 
         <img src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/money/dam/assets/150618111151-glastonbury-food-780x439.jpg" width="780" height="439" alt="glastonbury food" border="0" /> 
         <figcaption class="caption">
          There's a wide range of food available at Glastonbury.
         </figcaption> 
        </figure> 
        <p> </p> 
        <p> Once you add it all up, a trip to Glastonbury 2015 can give you sticker shock, with costs running into the thousands. </p> 
        <p> It's especially painful when you compare it to 1970, when entrance to the first festival cost &pound;1, which works out to &pound;15 ($24) once you take inflation into account. </p> 
        <p> Back then, the 1,500 festival goers enjoyed free milk from the local farm as part of their ticket. </p> 
        <p> <a href="http://money.cnn.com/2015/04/17/pf/millennials-coachella-costs/index.html"><span class="inStoryHeading">Related: Coachella is getting seriously expensive</span></a> </p> 
        <p> <a href="http://money.cnn.com/2015/02/26/luxury/luxury-travel-kids/"><span class="inStoryHeading">Related: Luxury getaways for (much) younger travelers</span></a> </p>
